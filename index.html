I am Tanusree Sharma- 3rd year PhD student in the Informatics at School of Information Sciences in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working with Professor Yang Wang at SALT LAB

My research interest is in human-centered research in technology innovation for usability,, accessibility by preventing privacy violations. The aim is to automate the process of designing and developing privacy decision making/ecommender tooling. Its a combination of qualitative and quantitative methods to tackle arising security and privacy risks in blockchain technology, De-Fi, Cloud/AI Technology.

As a researcher, I leverage large scale Machine Learning to understand end users and developers at scale to design and develop automated tooling for end users and developers. I work in learning and applying fundamentally new capability of engineering method within user experience research to strengthen privacy technologies for being more actionable, understandable, and available to users.
